Amazon + Flipkart Photoshoot Brief

Simple product photos. No confusion.

Give this page to the photographer. Follow these exact rules for clean marketplace listing images.

1:1

Square images

Use square frame for all listing images.

2000+

Pixel target

Export 2000 × 2000 px. Minimum 1000 px on longest side.

85%

Product size

Product should fill around 85% of the frame.

Main Image Layout

White background + centered product

The main image should show only the product on pure white background. No props. No text. No design.

Keep product inside 85% safe area
Main Image Checklist

Must follow

✓Background: pure white RGB 255,255,255 / #FFFFFF
✓Product straight, centered, sharp, and fully visible
✓Product fills around 85% of the image area
✓Real product color must stay accurate
✓Only show items included in the sale
Recommended size2000–3000 px longest side
Minimum size1000 px longest side
Maximum sizeBelow 10,000 px per side
File sizeUnder 10 MB
Color profilesRGB
Preferred formatJPEG / JPG
Required Shot List

Capture these product images

Main image must stay clean. Secondary images can show details, packaging, lifestyle, and feature callouts.

1. Front View

Main image. White background.

2. Back View

Show back label and details.

3. Side View

Show product depth and shape.

4. 45° Angle

Premium secondary hero shot.

5. Label Close-up

6. Texture Close-up

Show material, finish, or surface.

7. Packaging

Show box/pack only if included.

8. Lifestyle

Use-case image for secondary slot.

9. Size Reference

Show actual scale honestly.

10. Dimension Image

Clean measurements in secondary image.

11. Pack Contents

All items customer receives.

12. Detail Macro

High-sharpness detail shot.

Correct

Good main image

White background, large product, no text, no props, clean shadow, sharp focus.

Wrong

Do not shoot like this

No sale badge, no small product, no tilted bottle, no dirty/grey background, no heavy shadow.

SALE
Strict Don’ts

Never use in main image

×Text, discount, offer, badge, or graphic overlay
×Watermark, logo overlay, website, phone number, WhatsApp
×Extra props or items not included with product
×Blurry photo, low resolution, AI-looking edits
×Wrong color, over-saturation, unrealistic retouching
Shoot Setup

Photographer instructions

✓Use soft diffused light, not harsh direct flash
✓Clean product before every shot
✓Check focus at 100% zoom before finalizing
✓Keep same lighting and size across all images
✓Retouch dust only; do not change product shape/color
Final Delivery

Files photographer should deliver

✓12 edited JPG files in 2000 × 2000 px
✓All files under 10 MB each
✓sRGB color profile
✓Clean filenames: 01_front, 02_back, 03_side, 04_angle, etc.
✓Keep original high-resolution master files separately

Source basis: Amazon Seller Central product image guidance and Squareshot Amazon product image dimension guide.

Final rule: product must look clean, premium, accurate, and exactly like what customer will receive.
